Help

To use app go to the history page and click the "Add" button. This will then bring up an entry form asking for the birdhouse number, date, your user id, the number of eggs found in the house, number of alive birds, number of dead birds, the type of bird, and if there are any cowbirds or if the box is damaged. You can also enter any comments you may have or a different species if other is selected. When done filling out the form, click the add button. Your entry should now display on the table.

To edit an entry, simply click the "edit" button next to the entry of your choice. Update the information as needed and click "submit". When deleting an entry, simply click the "delete" button. If an entry was successfully edited or deleted a success message will appear.

You can click on the map page to view a map of Hueston Woods. The graph page shows a graph of totals from previous years. Under the graph is a table that displays the total number of fledglings per species.
